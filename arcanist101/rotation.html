The first ability that you use is call of destiny which gives you a chance to draw cards. It does nothing for you besides that and maintaining adrenaline stack. After that you use scratch dealer into quadra accelerate. Scratch dealer is first because it makes the cooldowns line up closer and also has an attack speed boost with its final tripod that makes quadra accelerate faster. After that you use return for personal crit damage buff and party crit rate synergy. Celestial rain is the priority dps ability after that as it has the highest average damage. After that you use spiral edge twice to build up another set of stacks and use secret garden to deal damage. You can also use serendipity if you draw cull. Otherwise the average performance of secret garden will outperform serendipity.

Cards

Three-Headed Snake – Basic Attack changes into 3 directions for 16s. On hit, earn 1 stack. Damage to foes +100%. Damage to Challenge or lower monsters +400%.

Mayhem – For 30s, Atk. Speed +3% for 4s every time skills are used, up to 15%.

Twisted Fate – For 4s, your skill Damage either stays the same or increases by 40%.

Corrosion – For 30s ,gain a 30% chance of +10% Damage to the foe for 5s.

Ghost – Ignore collision with monsters. Move Speed +20% and Damage from foes -50% for 3 hits for 16s.

Cull – For 4s, Crit Rate for all skill attacks 100% and Crit Damage +50% when they hit 1 foe.

Balance – For 30s, your Stacking skills are enhanced and grant you 1 more stack with each hit.

Judgment – For 4s, Ruin skills always trigger the 4 stack effect, regardless of the actual number of stacks cast upon foes.

Moon – Cooldown -10% and MP Recovery Speed increase +20% for 30s.

Star – Recovers 100% MP. Remaining skills Cooldown -15% except for Awakening, Movement, and Stand Up skills.

Wheel of Fortune – The Cooldown of the next skill decreases by 100%.

Royal – Fills empty card slots. You can obtain up to 2 cards.

Arcanist can draw any of these 12 cards at any point. Most of these cards you will use immediately, but four of them you must save for bursting. These cards are Wheel of Fortune, Cull, Twisted Fate, and Judgement. If you draw any combination of these cards, you are holding a burst hand that you can use to deal large amounts of damage quickly
